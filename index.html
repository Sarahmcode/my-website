About Me

My name is Sarah Mallicote, and I am from Johnson City, TN. I am currently a junior at the University of Tennessee, majoring in Information Sciences. I enjoy writing and spending time with friends during my free time.

My favorite part about the University of Tennessee is attending the football and basketball games, as I am a big sports fan. Although I don't play any sports, I enjoy tennis for fun, and I used to do competitive dance.

My favorite holidays are Halloween and Christmas because I enjoy the vibe they bring. I have two older siblings who have already graduated college and are working. I look forward to seeing what my future holds.
